Understanding the 468 Rule

The 468 rule determines eligibility for continuous employment benefits under Hong Kong's Employment Ordinance. An employee qualifies if they meet either of these conditions over any 4 consecutive weeks:

Method 1: Weekly Hours

Work 17 or more hours per week for 4 consecutive weeks

Method 2: Aggregate Hours

Work a total of 68 or more hours over any 4 consecutive weeks
